About Me

Biostatistician | Data Analyst

Hello! I am Stellamarries Syombua , a Biostatistician and a skilled Data Analyst passionate in discovering insightful findings from complex datasets. My expertise is more biased in applying statistical methods in public health, with a major focus on Cancer Research.

I'm currently working on Projects engaged in analyzing the clinical aspects of Esophageal and Breast Cancers. My enthusiasm for Bioinformatics involves my interest in integrating computational methods with biological data to advance research and healthcare innovation.

With strengths in data quality assuarance, statistical modelling and evidence-based decision-making, I am committed to impactful research. As a continuous learner, I am expanding my expertise in fields like time series analysis and advanced bioinformatics for analytical precision with a dedication to solve real-world problems.

Latest Projects

Breast Cancer Analysis in Meru

Conducted an in-depth analysis of breast cancer patient records to identify trends and associations between age, BMI, Ki67, and cancer subtypes. Focused on accurate data categorization and imputation of missing values to ensure high-quality insights.

Esophageal Cancer in Garissa

A comprehensive genomic and clinical profiling project utilizing whole exome sequencing to study esophageal cancer cases in Garissa. The project explores associations between socio-demographic factors, risk factors, and clinical outcomes to inform public health interventions.
